Privacy Policy — Sales Tax Saver

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Sales Tax Saver ("the app", "we", "us") helps you estimate the cheapest realistic way to buy an item — ship it home vs. drive to a lower-tax pickup — by factoring in sales tax, gas, and your time. This policy explains what information the app handles and what it does not.

The short version: No account, no sign-in. We don't run ads, analytics, or third-party tracking. We don't sell or share your data. Your settings and saved comparisons stay on your device. Your location is used only to look up the sales-tax rate for your area, and is never tied to your identity.

Information the app handles

  • Location. If you use the "My location" feature, the app reads your device's location (latitude/longitude) and sends those coordinates to a mapping service (BigDataCloud) to convert them into a ZIP code, so it can estimate your local sales-tax rate. Your coordinates are not stored by us and are not linked to any account or identity. The feature is optional — you can instead just type a ZIP or city, and the app works fully without location access.
  • On-device settings & history. Your trip assumptions, item inputs, and the comparisons you act on are saved in your device's local storage (under sts:* keys) so the app remembers them. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you clear the app's data or uninstall it.
  • No personal information. The app does not ask for or collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, or any account credentials.

What we don't do

  • No advertising and no ad networks.
  • No analytics or behavioral tracking SDKs.
  • No selling, renting, or sharing of your data for marketing.
  • No cross-app or cross-site tracking.

Your choices

  • Location: deny or revoke location permission in your device settings at any time — the app still works; just type a ZIP or city instead.
  • Stored data: clear it by clearing the app's data (mobile) or your browser site data (web), or by uninstalling the app.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Data security & retention

Because the app keeps your settings and history only on your own device and does not maintain user accounts or a server-side database of your data, you remain in control of it. Network requests to the services above use encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.
